## Natural Capital & Ecosystem Assessment

Various coordinated research-and-development datasets of England's land, freshwater, and coastal ecosystems. Aiming to produce a baseline of natural assets by 2028 following an ongoing five-year survey. Data first became available in 2025. Parnered with several envioronmental agencies and organisations. Combines remote and ground surveying. Surveyors are sampling 2500 1km^2 grid squares across, recording information on plants, habitats, hedgerows, streams and rivers, trees outside of woodlands, ponds, landscape, and soils.

- **Category:** Climate & Weather
- **Secondary Category:** Water & Hydrology
- **Provider:** Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs
- **Licensing:** Open
- **Data link 1:** [Data link 1](https://environment.data.gov.uk/natural-capital-ecosystem-assessment/classifier-search?level=1)
- **Data link 2:** 
- **Docs link:** [Docs link](https://environment.data.gov.uk/natural-capital-ecosystem-assessment/about)



## Recorded Flood Outlines

Extents of floods in England and Wales since 1946, and sometimes prior. Includes dates, durations, flood types/categories and causes, when known. Updated quarterly.

- **Provider:** Environment Agency

## Hydrology API

Hydrology readings from nearly 8000 river monitoring stations across England. Includes daily and subdaily recordings for various related metrics including flow, level, rainfall, groundwater level, pH, chlorophyll level etc. Different stations monitor different metrics, and historic data varies by station.

- **Category:** Water & Hydrology
- **Secondary Category:** Climate & Weather
- **Provider:** Environment Agency
- **Licensing:** Open
- **Data link 1:** [Data link 1](https://environment.data.gov.uk/hydrology/doc/reference#api-summary)
- **Data link 2:** [Data link 2](https://environment.data.gov.uk/hydrology/doc/reference#batch-api)

## National Forest Inventory GB

All forest and woodland area over 0.5 hectare with a minimum of 20% canopy cover, or the potential to achieve it, and a minimum width of 20 metres. This includes new planting, clearfell, windblow and restock. A new version is produced annually. Detected from Sentinel 2 imagery. There is some categorisation of forest type. It is a large dataset which can be quite unweildy.

- **Category:** Vegetation
- **Secondary Category:** Climate & Weather
- **Provider:** Forestry Commission
- **Licensing:** Open
- **Data link 1:** [Data link 1](https://data-forestry.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets/5a3efa283e81431b911b5c9872527ca5_0/explore)
- **Data link 2:** 
- **Docs link:** [Docs link](https://environment.data.gov.uk/dataset/0dbabff6-588e-44b3-a316-6353c4e47025)
